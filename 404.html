About Allocate
Your time is the only thing you can't get back.

Allocate is built for college students juggling coursework, employees managing packed schedules, gym-goers trying to stay consistent, and anyone who has ever reached the end of a week wondering where all the time went.

Whether you're carrying 18 credit hours, working 40+ hours a week, or chasing a side goal while life pulls you in six directions — this tool helps you map your hours, protect your free time, and actually follow through on what matters.

"It takes roughly 1,000 hours of focused, deliberate practice to go from a complete beginner to genuinely skilled at something. That's not a warning — it's a map."
Based on deliberate practice research — Anders Ericsson

1,000 hours feels like a mountain until you do the math. One hour a day gets you there in under three years. Two hours, under two. Most people never start because they're waiting for a perfect block of free time that never comes.

Imagine if instead of wishing you were good at something — you just chased it. Even for an hour a day. What could you become in a year?

Start mapping your time. The only thing standing between you and 1,000 hours is the decision to begin.
